    Fairwinds Yacht Profiler



    Previous Next

    A website that was made for a client as part of my industry project course where I was the lead programmer. It utilizes a vector based handicapping system to calculate an approximation of the time a yacht should take to complete a race given the different angles into or away from the wind during different stages and displays this data dynamically in 2 separate graphs and a table, all of which can be edited by a user.


    CAH 2.0



    Previous Next

    This is a game I made during the course of a weekend and is based off of the Cards Against Humanity game. I made it so me and my friends could mess around and have some fun with our own rules to the game. It is entirely network based and works with anyone who has a copy of the file anywhere around the world. The aim is to answer the question written in the bar at the top in a way that will most likely be chosen by the current host, the host will see a bunch of boxes that they can choose from and will not know who's answer is who's. Once the host picks an answer a point will be awarded to the owner of the answer and the next round will start with a new person as the host, this will continue until 1 person reaches 10 points.


    Colony Builder



    This project is one I have spent quite some time on. It involves you playing as the leader of a group of settlers in harsh foreign terrain where it is your goal to ensure the survival and happiness of you little settlers. As the leader you are allowed to command jobs that the settlers will complete such as setting rocks to be mined and walls to be build or trees to be cut down etc... . Each AI has some intelligence behind it and can accomplish a variety of things as well as has its own little personality and emotions.


    Grapple Race



    Previous Next

    Another network based game I made in my spare time. This one is based off of the steam game speed runners, where it the goal to run around the map while avoiding elimination via hazards and the like. The player is also given a grappling hook to help them traverse the map.
